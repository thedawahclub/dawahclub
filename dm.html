TYPE ONE: GENERAL DAWAH PAMPHLET

As part or dawah material please hand out one of each to every sister/brother of our humanity. Everyone gets these 2 pamphlets whether it is a street dawah, dawah table or stall, or when someone ask you about information on Islam.
* 1. Short Introduction To Islam.
* 2. Short Introduction To The Quran.

TYPE TWO: SOME ISSUE SPECIFIC PAMPHLETS

These pamphlets should never be distributed just by itself. These should always be given in addition to the two above general pamphlets.
