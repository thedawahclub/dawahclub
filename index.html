WHO DOES DAWAH
Every Muslim man and woman, young and old.
We need to remember that we get to live once in preparation for the life of eternity
As Allah (swt) said in Quran, (1)By Al-'Asr (the time). (2) Verily! Man is in loss, (3) Except those who believe (in Islamic Monotheism) and do righteous good deeds, and recommend one another to the truth [i.e. order one another to perform all kinds of good deeds (Al-Ma'ruf) which Allah has ordained, and abstain from all kinds of sins and evil deeds (Al-Munkar) which Allah has forbidden], and recommend one another to patience (for the sufferings, harms, and injuries which one may encounter in Allah's Cause during preaching His religion of Islamic Monotheism or Jihad, etc.). (surah al-Asr 103).
** SO, there are four conditional characteristics that man/woman must have in order to be saved from eternal loss. These are:
(1) BELIEVE +
(2) DO RIGHTEOUS GOOD DEEDS +
(3) ADVICE/RECOMMEND ONE ANOTHER TO THE TRUTH (Allah, Islam, good deeds etc.) +
(4) HAVE PATIENCE/PERSEVERANCE (while preaching the truth).


HOW TO DO DAWAH
It is easy. One may start on a very Basic/Individual level dawah activity and move on to Active/Community level dawah activity.

**Basic/Individual level dawah
** Every one of us should try to convey the message of Islam to people around us.
** Navigate through this website or through other means and try to learn as much as you can. Then take some time from your busy schedule and go out on sidewalk or a Park or a parking lot and spread the message of Islam by talking and distributing dawah materials (pamphlets, Quran etc).
** Talk or distribute pamphlets about Islam to your neighbors, classmates, co-workers, teachers and strangers. Also sign-up, communicate, participate and cooperate with Dawah Club (or Dawah Group) in your local community or Masjid.

**Active/Community level dawah
** In this level every individual is required to go through all required materials in this website and master those materials.
** Then work with the local Masjid (or community) to start a Dawah Club in the Masjid. Individuals of this Club will organize and lead dawah activities in their local community.
** To start a Dawah Club (or Dawah Group) in your Masjid use materials and resources from this website. For more information or help with starting a Dawah Club/Group please contact us via email.
